🚨 Important Safety Notice

HearMee is not a crisis service and does not provide medical or psychiatric care.

If you are in immediate danger or experiencing a medical emergency:

  • Call 911 (U.S.)
  • Call or text 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline – U.S.)
  • Or contact your local emergency number

If you are outside the U.S., please contact your country’s emergency services.

💬 Frequently Asked Questions

Is HearMee therapy?

No. HearMee is an AI-powered emotional support tool. It does not diagnose, treat, or replace licensed mental health professionals.

Is HearMee anonymous?

Yes. HearMee does not require real names and is designed to protect user identity.

What does the AI do?

The AI chatbot (Ami) listens, validates feelings, and offers trauma-informed emotional support. It avoids giving medical or clinical advice and encourages users to seek trusted support when appropriate.

Can HearMee contact emergency services for me?

No. HearMee cannot detect your location or contact emergency services. If you are in immediate danger, call your local emergency number.

Who is HearMee for?

HearMee is designed for adolescents and young adults seeking private emotional support.
